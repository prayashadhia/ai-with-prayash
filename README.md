## The channel

AI moves fast. This channel keeps you caught up — the model launches, the tools, the news that actually matters — in tight, 60-second Shorts made for everyone, not just engineers.

Every Short is **fact-checked against original sources**, so it stays punchy without the hype.

## Built with AI

Here's the fun part: an AI-powered workflow does the heavy lifting. It scans the day's AI news, checks the facts, and drafts each script — then I review, polish, and publish.

It means more Shorts, fewer errors, and a human still making the final call.

**What makes it agentic:** the AI doesn't follow a fixed script. It decides what to fact-check, researches on its own, and keeps digging until it can confirm or drop each claim — the same loop a careful human researcher would run, just automated.
